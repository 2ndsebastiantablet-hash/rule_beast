# Rule Beast VR Sound Direction

## Sonic Palette
Minimal horror-electronic palette: sub bass drones, reversed metallic breaths, distant impacts, electrical puzzle chirps, and sharp distorted monster stingers. Audio should heighten spatial dread without masking important cues.

## Music Direction
Loopable instrumental background bed, low-to-medium energy, tense and mysterious. Slow pulsing synth bass, faint industrial percussion, bowed metal textures, and sparse high glass tones. It should support 10 escalating rounds and avoid melodic distraction.

## SFX Language
Puzzle interaction: clean electric ticks and rising confirmation tones. Round advance: ritual gong plus digital alarm. Monster attack/kill: short claw impact with red distorted growl. UI: subdued visor clicks. Footstep/heartbeat tension can be procedural.

## Mix And Playback Notes
Start audio only after a player gesture. Keep music quiet under gameplay, SFX punchy, and provide a mute toggle. Use generated MP3s for the main loop and signature cues, with simple procedural beeps for rapid UI feedback.
